Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our internship program

Our program is designed to help students gain practical knowledge in programming through hands-on assignments. By completing tasks in various domians, students will receive internship certificates that can help them stand out in their future career endeavors.

To earn your internship certificate, simply complete the required assignments and projects for the selected domain. Once you've successfully submitted the assignments, we will verify them, and you will receive your certificate.

The program is designed to be completed in 4 weeks. During this time, you'll work on projects at your own pace. You'll have the full support of our team, and upon completing the assignments within the 4-week timeframe, you'll receive your internship certificate.
